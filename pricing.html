FAQ

Common questions.

Can I switch between monthly and yearly at any time? +
Yes. You can switch billing cycles any time. When upgrading to yearly, you'll be credited for unused monthly days. Downgrading to monthly takes effect at the next renewal date.
What counts as a "honeypot node"? +
One running instance of main.py listening on a port. You can run multiple nodes on the same machine or across different servers — each process counts as one node.
Do I have to use your cloud LLM, or can I bring my own? +
Sentinel lets you swap any HuggingFace checkpoint into the on-device agent via ONDEVICE_MODEL=. Hunter adds CLOUD_API_URL= so you can point the fallback at your own Novita endpoint, Ollama instance, or any OpenAI-compatible API.
Is there a trial for paid plans? +
Yes — both Sentinel and Hunter come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required at sign-up. If you cancel before day 14, you owe nothing.
What happens to my logs if I cancel? +
You have 30 days after cancellation to export your logs in JSONL or CSV. After that, data is purged from our servers. Scout logs live entirely on your own machine, so nothing is ever stored by us.
Is HydraPoT legal to run? +
Honeypots are legal in most jurisdictions when run on infrastructure you own or control. You are responsible for complying with local laws. We recommend placing the honeypot on an isolated VLAN and notifying your network admin before deployment.
